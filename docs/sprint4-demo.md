### 2 — Financial Reports (90 sec)

[**Financial Reports** in sidebar]

*"Four reports — all from approved entries including the adjustment we just posted."*

[**Trial Balance** → today → **Update**]

*"Total debits equal total credits."*

[**Save Snapshot** → back to Reports → show saved row]

[**Income Statement** → year-to-date → **Update**]

*"Revenue minus expenses equals net income."*

[**Balance Sheet** → **Update**]

*"Assets equal liabilities plus equity."*

[**Retained Earnings** → **Update**]

*"Opening RE plus net income equals ending balance."*

[**Print / Download PDF** on any report. Then **Email Report** → pick manager → **Send**]

*"Reports can be saved, printed, or emailed as PDFs."*

[**Chart of Accounts** → click an account **number** → lands on ledger. Back → click into any account → **Email Manager/Admin** → send]

*"Account names and numbers link to the ledger. Accountants can email managers from any account page."*
